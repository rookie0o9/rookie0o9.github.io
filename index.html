BIO

Welcome to my blog! Here you’ll find a blend of professional insights, personal reflections, and the projects I’m most excited about.

Background & Interests: I’m Ade, based in London, UK, and I was raised in a family where a mix of cultures, faiths and perspectives shaped my curiosity about the wider world. That love of diversity still drives me today, whether I’m exploring markets in Egypt or relaxing on the beaches of the Benin Republic. Outside of work, you’ll often find me reading (I’m currently diving into Mastery by Robert Greene), capturing everyday moments through photography or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. These hobbies give me space to recharge and spark creativity.

Professional Journey: I’m a 2nd Line Support Engineer transitioning into Incident Response with a Master’s in Applied Cybersecurity. Over the last decade I’ve built and secured Microsoft 365 environments, migrated organisations from third‑party platforms to Exchange Online and SharePoint Online, rolled out Intune/Autopilot and modern device management, and implemented MFA and Conditional Access to harden user identities. I’ve also triaged and contained threats using Microsoft 365 Defender, applying KQL and audit log analysis to isolate compromised accounts and devices. I value clear communication, strong documentation and collaboration, and I enjoy turning one‑off fixes into repeatable solutions through runbooks and knowledge bases.

I’m always looking for new opportunities to blend technical excellence with a human touch and to help teams deliver secure, resilient IT services. Feel free to browse my projects to see what I’m currently working on, and thanks for stopping by!projects
