BIO

Welcome to my blog! Here you'll find a glimpse into my professional and personal life along with interests.

Background: My name is Ade, and I'm delighted to welcome you to my personal blog. I come from a multi ideological family, with different beliefs and outlook on life. Growing up, I learned to appreciate and celebrate the diverse perspectives, opinions, traditions and customs of family and friends, which has shaped my outlook on life. One of my biggest passions is exploring new places and cultures. I love to travel and have been fortunate enough to visit some amazing destinations around the world. Some of my favorite trips have been to Egypt and Benin Republic.

In my free time, I enjoy a variety of hobbies such as reading ( currently reading Mastery by Robert Greene ), photography, and cooking. I find that these activities allow me to unwind and express my creativity.

Professional Journey: My passion for IT security, combined with my extensive knowledge of business practices has driven my desire to help organizations achieve their security objectives. Experienced and proactive IT professional with a Master’s in Applied Cybersecurity and a strong foundation in administrative management. Specializes in cybersecurity analysis, IT support, and systems management. Adept at leveraging collaborative approaches and a development mindset to deliver high-quality strategic solutions. Seeking to bring analytical acumen and a record of success in cyber resilience to a dynamic team.
