BIO

Welcome to my blog! Here you'll find a glimpse into my professional and personal life along with interests.

I come from a multi ideological family, with different religious and personal beliefs. Growing up, I learned to appreciate and celebrate the diverse perspectives, opinions, traditions and customs of my family members, which has shaped my outlook on life. One of my biggest passions is exploring new places and cultures. I love to travel and have been fortunate enough to visit some amazing destinations around the world. Some of my favorite trips have been to Egypt and Benin Republic.

Egypt: I was in awe of the ancient pyramids, hieroglyphics, and the bustling streets of Cairo. I also had the opportunity to take a boat ride along the Nile River and visit Luxor, Karnak, and Abu Simbel temples.

Benin Republic: My trip to here was an eye-opening experience. I learned about the country's rich history and culture, visited the voodoo capital of Ouidah, and explored the bustling markets in Cotonou. It was an incredible journey that opened my eyes to a whole new world. I hope to visit Greece, France, and Morocco next.

In my free time, I enjoy a variety of hobbies such as reading ( currently reading Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications by Richard Szeliski ), photography, and cooking. I find that these activities allow me to unwind and express my creativity.

Professional Journey: I have 4+ years' experience in IT Security configuring, monitoring and managing infrastructures. I am also passionate about IT and determined to learn, develop, and progress in the field. A team player with high motivation levels, always ready to contribute ideas and tackle challenges. Proficient in Azure, AWS, GCP, Bitdefender, Crowdstrike, Checkpoint, Fortinet, VPN & SSL, SIEM, Nessus, MobileIron, Cisco Meraki, Pen Testing, PSN, AD, DNS, DHCP, Door Access Control, Security Change Management, Vulnerability Management, Cyber Security awareness training tool, Ransomware Simulator, Virus Total, Security Auditing, Jira, and Microsoft Defender for Cloud.

Feel free to check out my current projects.

On this blog, I'll be sharing my experiences and insights on these topics and more. Thank you for joining me on this journey!
